Hi John,

Thank you for reaching out and for the detailed information about the steps you've already tried.

I've looked into your account and can see the connection attempts. It appears there's a known issue with Chase's Plaid integration that our engineering team is currently working on.

In the meantime, could you please try the following:

  1. Go to Settings > Connected Accounts
  2. Click "Add Account" and select "Manual Entry"
  3. Choose Chase from the list and follow the OAuth flow

This alternative method bypasses Plaid and connects directly through Chase's API.

Let me know if this works for you!
